Contrary to popular belief,Lore Ipsum is not simply random text.It has roots in a piece of classical Latin literature from 45 BC, making it over 2000 yaers old. Richard McClintock,
a Latin professor at Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia, looked up on of the more obscure Latin words,consectetur,from a Lorem Ipsum passage,and going through the cites of
the word in classic literature,discovered the undoubtable source.Lorem Ipsum comes from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The extremes of
Good and Evil) by Cicero,written in 45 BC. This book is a treatise on the theory of ethics,very popular during the Renaissance. The first line og Lorem Ipsum,"Lorem Ipsum dolor sit
amet..",comes from a line in section 1.10.32.
